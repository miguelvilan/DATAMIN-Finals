# Data Dictionary

**Data Dictionary** — This sheet describes the variables included in the OBS dataset, including variable names/patterns, definitions, data types, value coding, and methodological notes.

*Users should review the README tab for important comparability notes and methodological caveats.*

---
**Quick reference: common variable prefixes**
- q* — Scored OBS survey indicators
- gq* — General questions (unscored; contextual use)
- obi / *_obi — Transparency and oversight indices and related components
- round* — Round-level composite values
- grade_* / *_cat* — Categorical groupings used in reporting

## OBS Data 2006-2025 Codebook
| Variable / Pattern| Description | Type | Values / Coding | Notes |
| :---: | :--- | :---: | :---: | :--- |
| year | OBS survey round year | Number | 2006 - 2025<br/>(in this launch) | Data are available from 2006 onward. If a country does not appear in a given year, it means the country was not included in the survey that year. |
| region | Broad regional classification | Text |
| rank | Country rank in the Open Budget Index (OBI) | Number | | Rankings are provisional in partial releases. Final rankings will be available only after all participating countries are published. |
| obi | Open Budget Index (OBI) transparency score | Number<br/>(0–100) | Average of q1–q102 and timeliness variables(t3pbs, t3ebp, t3eb, t3iyr, t3myr, t3yer, t3ar) | "Calculated as an unweighted average. roundobi is the OBI score rounded to the nearest whole number." |
| obi_cat | Category based on rounded transparency (OBI) score | Categorical | Scant or None;<br/>Minimal;<br/>Limited;<br/>Substantial;<br/>Extensive | Categories are based on the rounded OBI score:<br/>• 0–20: Scant or no information<br/>• 21–40: Minimal information<br/>• 41–60: Limited information<br/>• 61–80: Substantial information<br/>• 81–100: Extensive information |
| obi_cat2 | Simplified transparency category based on rounded OBI score | Categorical | Low;<br/>Limited;<br/>Sufficient | Categories are based on the rounded OBI score:<br/>• 0-40: Low<br/>• 41-60: Limited <br/>• 61-100: Sufficient |
| obi_cat3 | Binary transparency sufficiency category | Categorical | Insufficient;<br/>Sufficient | A score of 61 or higher for the OBI is considered sufficient. |
| obi_cat4 | Quintile classification of rounded OBI score | Categorical | Bottom Quintile;<br/>2nd Quintile;<br/>Middle Quintile;<br/>4th Quintile;<br/>Top Quintile | Categories are based on the rounded OBI score:<br/>• 0-20: Bottom Quintile <br/>• 21-40: 2nd Quintile <br/>• 41-60: Middle Quintile<br/>• 61-80: 4th Quintile <br/>• 81-100: Top Quintile |
| oversight_obi | Overall oversight score (legislature + SAI) | Number<br/>(0–100) | Unweighted average of q107–q124 | Do not compare oversight scores prior to 2017, as the questions and structure changed in earlier rounds. Roundoversight_obi is the rounded value. |
| leg_obi | Legislature oversight sub-score | Number<br/>(0–100) | Unweighted average of q107-q118 | Do not compare prior to 2017. Roundleg_obi is the rounded value. |
| sai_obi | Supreme Audit Institution (SAI) oversight sub-score | Number<br/>(0–100) | Unweighted average of q119-q124 | Do not compare prior to 2017. Roundsai_obi is the rounded value. |
| ifi_obi | Independent Fiscal Institution (IFI) sub-score | Number<br/>(0–100) | Unweighted average of q103-q106 | Exploratory indicator assessing Independent Fiscal Institutions (IFI).<br/>Not included in composite indices. roundifi_obi is the rounded value. |
| grade_oversight,<br/>grade_leg,<br/>grade_sai,<br/>grade_ifi | Category based on rounded oversight sub-scores | Categorical | Weak;<br/>Limited;<br/>Adequate | • 0-40: Weak<br/>• 41-60: Limited <br/>• 61-100: Adequate |
| pub_eng | Public participation score | Number<br/>(0–100) | Unweighted average of q125-q142 | Do not compare participation scores prior to 2017 due to questionnaire changes. roundpub_eng is the rounded value. |
| grade_pub_eng | Public participation category based on rounded score | Categorical | Few or no opportunities;<br/>Limited opportunities;<br/>Adequate opportunities | • 0-40: Few or no opportunities<br/>• 41-60: Limited opportunities<br/>• 61-100: Adequate opportunities |
| pbs, ebp, eb, cb, iyr, myr, yer, ar | Publication status of each key budget document | Categorical |• PW: Published online in a timely manner<br/>• HC: Produced but available only in hard/soft copy (not online)<br/>• PL: Published late (outside internationally accepted timeframe)<br/>• IU: Produced for internal use only• NP: Not produced | These variables indicate whether a document meets OBS public availability standards and if not what the status of the document is (not produced, hard copy, etc.). |
| pub_pbs, pub_ebp, pub_eb, pub_cb, pub_iyr, pub_myr, pub_yer, pub_ar | Binary indicator of online availability | Binary | 0 = Not available online;<br/>1 = Available online | Binary indicator of whether the document was publicly available online within internationally accepted timeframe (1) or not (0). |
| obi_pbs, obi_ebp, obi_eb, obi_cb, obi_iyr, obi_myr, obi_yer, obi_ar | Transparency sub-score for each key budget document | Number<br/>(0–100) | • Pre-Budget Statement: PBS- 2 (t3pbs) and q54-q58<br/>• Executive's Budget Proposal: EBP-2 (t3ebp) and q1-q53<br/>• Enacted Budget: EB-2 (t3ebp) and q59-q63<br/>• Citizens Budget: q64-q67<br/>• In-Year Reports: IYRs-2 (t3iyr) and q68-q75<br/>• Mid-Year Review: MYR-2 (t3myr) and q76-q83<br/>• Year-End Report: YER-2 (t3yer) and q84-q96<br/>• Audit Report: AR-2 (t3ar) and q97-q102 | Unweighted average of document-specific questions and related timeliness variable. roundobi_[doc] is the rounded value to the nearest whole number. |
| obi_pbs_cat, obi_ebp_cat, obi_eb_cat, obi_cb_cat, obi_iyr_cat, obi_myr_cat, obi_yer_cat, obi_ar_cat | Category based on rounded document sub-score | Categorical  | Scant or no information; Minimal information; Limited information; Substantial information; Extensive information | Category are based on rounded OBI sub-score:<br/>• 0-20: Scant or no information<br/>• 21-40: Minimal information<br/>• 41-60: Limited information<br/>• 61-80: Substantial information<br/>• 81-100: Extensive information |
| pbs2, ebp2, eb2, iyrs2, myr2, yer2, ar2 | Letter response used to determine document timeliness | Text | a/b/c/d | These letter responses determine the timeliness score (t3 scores) used in transparency calculations.<br/>Scoring conversion:<br/>• a = 100<br/>• b = 67<br/>• c = 33<br/>• d = 0 (not publicly available online or published late) |
| pbs5a, ebp5a, eb5a, iyr5a, myr5a, yer5a, ar5a, cb5a | Letter response used to determine document publication status | Text | • a: Published late - after the internationally accepted timeframe<br/>• b: Produced but only available in hard/soft copy (not available online)<br/>• c: Produced for internal purposes/use only<br/>• d: Not produced at all<br/>• e: Not applicable (the document is publicly available) | The letter response used to determine publication status (PW, PL, HC, IU, NP). |
| q1–q142 | Indicator scores from the OBS questionnaire | Number<br/>(0–100) | 0, 33, 67, 100 | Use the Metadata_UI tab to view question text and evidence fields. Do not compare individual questions prior to 2015 without reviewing the corresponding questionnaire. Blank cells typically indicate “Not applicable” and are excluded from calculations. |
| t3pbs, t3ebp, t3eb, t3iyr, t3myr, t3yer, t3ar | Timeliness variables for key budget documents | Categorical | t3pbs (PBS-2),<br/>t3ebp (EBP-2),<br/>t3eb (EB-2),<br/>t3iyr (IYRs-2),<br/>t3myr (MYR-2),<br/>t3yer (YER-2),<br/>t3ar (AR-2) | See questionnaire for response options. These variables are included in transparency calculations. |
| new25 | Binary indicator of a country included in the OBS 2025 for the first time | Binary | 0 = Included prior to 2025;<br/>1 = New for 2025 | Indicates whether a country was included for the first time in OBS 2025. |
| comp0608, comp0610, comp0612, comp0810, comp0812, comp1012, comp1215, comp1517, comp1719, comp1921,comp2123, comp2325 | Binary indicators identifying countries comparable across survey rounds | Binary |0 = Not comparable;<br/><br/>1 = Comparable for the indicated time period | Use these variables when analyzing trends over time, filter to countries where the relevant compXXXX variable equals 1.<br/><br/>Example:<br/>To analyze trends from 2006 onward, keep only countries where comp0612 = 1.<br/><br/>comp0612 – identifies the 2 countries comparable from OBS 2006 to present<br/>comp0812 – identifies the 5 countries comparable from OBS 2008 to present<br/>comp1012 – identifies the 5 countries comparable from OBS 2010 to present<br/>comp1215 – identifies the 5 countries comparable from OBS 2012 to present<br/>comp1517 – identifies the 5 countries comparable from OBS 2015 to present<br/>comp1719 – identifies the 5 countries comparable from OBS 2017 to present<br/>comp1921 – identifies the 5 countries comparable from OBS 2019 to present<br/>comp2123 - identifies the 5 countries comparable from OBS 2021 to present<br/>comp2325 - identifies the 6 countries comparable from OBS 2023 to present |
| Q1LETTER-Q142LETTER | Letter responses corresponding to indicator scores (available in Scores + Letters tab) | Text | a/b/c/d/e | Data for letters is only present since 2017 OBS as questions as the structure of the questions changed prior to that. Most indicators will have five response options (A through E), while some will have three (A through C) and depending on the option have different scoring conversions.<br/><br/>Five-option indicators:<br/>• a = 100<br/>• b = 67<br/>• c = 33, <br/>• d = 0<br/>• e = Not applicable (and thus not counted)<br/><br/>Three-option indicators:<br/>• a = 100<br/>• B = 0<br/>• C = Not applicable (and thus not counted) |
